Tokenomics

BSCDOG uses the dynamic RFI frictionless staking mechanism to reward holders. Each transaction incurs a 3% charge which is then redistributed to all token holders proportional to the amount of BSCDOG held. This is designed to reward hodlers the longer you hold the more your wallet grows. 50% of the Total supply was burned to the black hole on launch. Current and now max supply 500,000,000,000,000 BSCDOG. 1% of the tokens to cover the fees for the developer. The initial liquidity tokens have also been burned. By launching on Binance Smart Chain it enables users to avoid the excessive fees of the Ethereum Network. Bringing fun back to trading. Frequent buys and sells reward the holders of the token!
Fair Launch

BSCDOG was a stealth launch with no presale. 1% of supply went to the dev to cover fees. The initial liquidity was added by the dev and the project was given to the community.

Frictionless Staking

3% of every transaction redistributed to holders. Earn yield by just holding! Max supply now 500,000,000,000,000 BSCDOG

SAFE
